How to send people messages.

  1. 1.1 Type the person's name into the recipient box at the left of the compose message page.
  2. 1.2 Results for your search will show up as you type in the recipient box.
  3. 1.3 Click on the person's name when you see it

  1. 2.Type your message into the message content box at the left of the compose message page.

  1. 3.Click the send button to send the message.
